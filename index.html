About Me

Hey, I'm Om Gupta — a video editor, motion graphic designer, and content strategist with over 3 years of experience helping brands tell their story through video.

I edit everything from short-form reels and podcasts to ad films, VSLs, and brand films. I also work on UGC-style ads, thumbnails, and content repurposing — basically, if it's video, I've got you covered.

I've worked with brands like Shoppers Stop, Trove Experiences, Truffle Nation, and many more. Whether you need a scroll-stopping reel or a full video strategy, I'm here to make it happen. Let's work together!
